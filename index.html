Our Story

About RareMed Supply

We exist for one reason — to make sure no patient in Nigeria ever has to suffer because they could not find their medicine.

Our Mission

Connecting Every Nigerian Patient to the Medicine They Need — in Seconds

We built RareMed Supply because patients across Nigeria are spending hours — sometimes days — going from pharmacy to pharmacy looking for essential, rare, or specialty medications. Meanwhile, pharmacies with exactly those medicines in stock sit waiting for patients who never find them. We exist to close that gap.

📖

Our Story

RareMed Supply was founded in Nigeria out of a deeply personal understanding of the frustration patients feel when they cannot find life-saving medicines. After witnessing patients in Nigeria struggle to locate specific drugs — sometimes driving hours across cities — we knew technology could solve this problem. We built a platform that connects the two sides of this gap in real time.

⚡

What We Do

RareMed Supply is a real-time drug availability platform. Patients search for any medication and instantly see which verified pharmacies near them have it in stock — with prices, distances, and direct contact. Pharmacies list their inventory and connect with patients who need them. Every listing is verified. Every pharmacy is screened. No ads. No middlemen.
